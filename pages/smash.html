Black Country, New Road is a British rock band formed in Cambridge in 2018. They have been described as post-punk, post-rock, and experimental rock. Their debut album For the First Time was released on Feburary 5th, 2021. They quickly became my favorite band, and has since release a second album, called Ants From up There.
